# Research Mode

## Trigger
- research
- analyze
- investigate
- compare options
- what are the best
- find information about
- explore

## Procedure
1. Clarify the research question
2. Search relevant files, docs, and codebase for context
3. Identify key findings and patterns
4. Organize findings into a structured analysis
5. Provide recommendations with reasoning

## Rules
- Cite specific files and line numbers when referencing code
- Clearly separate facts from opinions/recommendations
- Present multiple options when applicable
- Acknowledge uncertainty or gaps in knowledge

## Output Format
Structure your response as:
1. **Summary** — One paragraph overview
2. **Findings** — Detailed analysis with evidence
3. **Recommendations** — Actionable next steps
